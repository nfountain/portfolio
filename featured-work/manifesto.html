Live your life.

Life is an adventure, take new roads, run new trails.

Indulge your curiosity.

Always dream.

Take time to create good memories.

Build traditions that you want to last. They are a touchstone. A source of joy, connection, continuity, and comfort in tough times.

Love well. That includes yourself, those who matter, and anything that is worthy.

Savor the joy life brings.

Challenge your fears, courage is a powerful friend.

Don't forget to play; life is bleak without fun, curiosity, and beauty.

Never stop learning. About life, about yourself, about things that interest you.

Choose what will matter years from now.

Explore new places, be they near or far.

Meet new people- be curious about them and take the time to talk to them. You will learn so much.

New experiences are worthwhile…

They can remind us of lessons that we forgot and expose us to new opportunities.

Live your life intentionally. It's the only one you get.
